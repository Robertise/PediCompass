# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Project discussions should stay professional, technical, and respectful. Good
contributions include clear reasoning, reproducible evidence, and willingness
to correct mistakes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, public disclosure
of private information, and knowingly sharing unsafe or misleading clinical
guidance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict access for
behavior that harms the project or its contributors.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers.
